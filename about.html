Our mission

Make it effortless for people to reconnect with their belongings. Post in seconds, search with powerful filters, and contact finders securely—no friction.

  • Simple reporting & clear status (Lost / Found)
  • Fast search by title, area, and type
  • Clean, mobile-first experience

Why local first?

Lost2Found raises visibility for posts from your selected area at the top of the feed, while keeping all other posts visible below. This “area-based priority” helps you see the most relevant items first without hiding the rest. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

How it works

1

Post

Create a lost or found post in seconds. Add a title, photo, description, and area so others can quickly identify the item.

2

Discover

Browse the feed or use search filters (item type, area, keywords) to find matching posts and increase your chances of reconnecting.

3

Reconnect

Open an item’s details and securely contact the poster to return a found item or claim your lost one. Fast, safe, and community-driven.

🔒 Trust & Clarity

🌍 Inclusive by Design

Posts from your chosen area appear first for relevance, but all other items remain visible. Everyone gets equal opportunity to be reunited with their belongings.

FAQ

Do I need an account to browse?

No—anyone can view posts. Creating an account lets you post and manage your items.

How does area priority work?

Pick your area once. Posts from that area appear first, others follow right after. :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}

What info should I include in a post?

Title, description, area, and a clear photo. Add when/where it was lost/found for faster matches. :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}
